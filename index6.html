About Our Church

Progressive Glory International Church is a place of worship, healing, and deliverance where lives are transformed through the power of God's Word.

Our Vision & Mission

At Progressive Glory International Church, we are committed to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ and helping people experience God's transformative power in their lives. Our mission is to raise disciples who will impact their world for Christ.

We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of God's Word, and the manifestation of the Holy Spirit's gifts. Our services are marked by passionate worship, powerful preaching, and life-changing encounters with God.

Whether you're seeking spiritual growth, healing, deliverance, or simply a community of believers to fellowship with, you're welcome at Progressive Glory International Church.

Our Spiritual Leader

Meet the man of God leading Progressive Glory International Church

Prophet Dr. Nicodemus

Senior Pastor & Founder

Prophet Dr. Nicodemus is a dynamic preacher, teacher, and prophet with a passion for seeing lives transformed through the power of God's Word. With over 20 years of ministry experience, he has dedicated his life to raising disciples and equipping believers for spiritual excellence.

Under his leadership, Progressive Glory International Church has grown into a vibrant ministry known for its emphasis on prayer, worship, and the prophetic.
